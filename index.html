Architecture

I am extremely passionate about not just building things, but building things the right way. I understand the maintaining and updating buggy code, I know how difficult it is to build on top of fragile code. I have always pushed for code to be written in a way that is extensible, maintainable and bug-free, even if it takes a little longer to do so.

Optimization

Doing things the right way is not enough: You have to make sure the thing works, but also works fast. Premature optimization is the root of all evil: But no optimization gives you 10 FPS. I love squeezing every bit of processing power possible - I try to eliminate redundancies and carefully plan out what I'm going to do and how I do it in order to do it in the best way possible.

Coding standards

I am a stickler for creating, following and preaching about coding conventions. I think uniformity and consistency are extremely important. Just like a well-written book, all the code in the codebase should read and feel the same, and one should try to maintain that at all times.
